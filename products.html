About company

BHOE LA DOHH means, "Let'ss go to Tibet" in . Our T-shirt desing's are illustrated and designed by talented Tibetan artist from New York, Canada and India. Our goal is to promote Tibetan arts and artists.

For every item purchased, we are donating 10% of fthe sales to Sambhota Sonada Hostel (C.S.T Sonada Darjeeling). Our donation will help Tibetan students for better education and living facilities.
